Poker

I have been playing poker for about 15 years. I started out having to bring the list of hands from best to worst. I have not had to do that for a long time. I have won two survivor tournaments at Parx Casino. I met some great people since I started to play poker and am proud of myself for being one of the most improved players.

Estate Planning

I started practicing law in 1991. I have been spending most of my time representing clients in divorce, custody and support cases. I have prepared many estate planning documents for clients and have enjoyed that work much more than the divorce, custody and support matters. At this stage in my career I would like to continue to prepare estate planning documents for clients and instead of representing clients in divorce, custody and support cases spend my time as a software developer.

Animals

I love animals. My favorite animals are tigers. I could watch them for hours without getting bored. My favorite animal will always be my dog, Scooby. Scooby was a Labrador Retriever. We had Scooby from 1998 to 2011 and I have his picture as a puppy as a popsocket on my cell phone. I will always miss him.
